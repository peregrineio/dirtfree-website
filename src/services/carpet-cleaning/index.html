What you get

The Carpet Cleaning process.

Truck-powered hot water extraction with patented organic cleaners removes embedded dirt, allergens, and spots. No harsh chemicals. No sticky residue that re-soils. Carpet dries in a fraction of the typical time and stays cleaner longer because there is no soap left in the fiber.

  • Truck-mounted power extractionDiesel-engine truck-mount unit generates ten times the suction of portable rental machines.
  • Patented organic cleanersSpecially formulated organic compounds clean to the padding without harsh chemicals or detergents.
  • 1 to 6 hour dry timeFree Speed Dry System cuts dry time from 24-48 hours to under six. Walk on your carpets the same day.
  • Safe for pets and childrenOur cleaners are so safe Aubrey demonstrated by drinking them on live TV. No fumes, no residue.
  • Soap residue removalWe pull the soap left behind by previous cleaners. That stops the rapid re-soiling cycle most homes have.
  • Seven-day callback windowIf a spot returns within a week of approved test spot, we come back at no charge.
