The Water Damage Restoration process.

Twenty-four seven response across Greater Houston. Industrial water extraction. Industrial drying. Mold prevention. We have walked into hundreds of flooded Houston homes since 1989. Hurricanes, tropical storms, slab leaks, washing-machine line failures. Same crew, same equipment, same fast response.

  • 24/7 availabilityCall any hour. We dispatch within minutes for active flooding.
  • Rapid water extractionTruck-powered extraction pulls water out of carpet, padding, and subfloor before mold sets in.
  • Industrial dryingAir movers and dehumidifiers dry the structure in days, not weeks.
  • Mold preventionAntimicrobial treatment on affected materials cuts off mold growth at the source.
  • Insurance documentationWe document moisture readings and damage extent for your insurance claim.
