What you get

The Upholstery process.

We clean every fabric type from microfiber and cotton to silk and leather (with the appropriate process). Spot pre-treatment, deodorizing, and optional fabric protection are included. Most pieces are dry within hours.

  • All fabric typesMicrofiber, cotton, polyester, linen, silk-blend. We test before we commit to a process.
  • Spot pre-treatmentStains and high-touch zones get pre-treatment with our patented organic cleaners before extraction.
  • Deodorizing includedPet, food, and body-oil odors come out at the molecular level. Not masked, removed.
  • Fabric protection optionAftercare protector adds stain resistance for the year ahead. Recommended on light fabrics.
  • Same-day dryTruck-powered extraction means furniture is usable the same evening, not 48 hours later.
  • Visible resultWe always start on the worst cushion or arm. You see the difference before we run the full piece.

Free test spot first. Always. We never expect you to pay before you see what we can do. The Dirt Free Promise
