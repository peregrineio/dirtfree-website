What you get

The Natural Stone Cleaning process.

Professional care for marble, travertine, granite, limestone, and slate. Polishing, honing, sealing, and scratch removal. We assess the stone first, custom quote, and use stone-specific chemistry. No vinegar, no off-the-shelf household cleaner.

  • All stone typesMarble, travertine, granite, limestone, slate, and engineered quartz.
  • Polishing and honingRestore shine on marble or move to honed matte finish on request.
  • Professional sealingAnnual or biennial seal protects against staining and etch.
  • Scratch removalLight surface scratches buffed out. Deep gouges quoted separately.
  • Stone-specific chemistryWe use stone-specific products. Most household cleaners actively damage marble.
  • Custom assessmentEvery stone job starts with an in-person assessment and a written quote.
