What you get

The Area & Oriental Rug Cleaning process.

We pick up your rug, hand wash or steam clean it depending on construction, treat the fringe, and deliver it back. Color-safe testing on every rug before any chemistry touches it. Free pickup and delivery within Greater Houston.

  • Hand washing availableHand-knotted, antique, and silk-blend rugs get hand wash treatment. Mechanical extraction for synthetics.
  • Fringe cleaningFringe is treated separately. No bleached or frayed edges from aggressive chemistry.
  • Color-safe testingEvery rug gets a color-fastness test before treatment. Antique dyes deserve respect.
  • Free pickup and deliveryWe pick up at your house and deliver back. Same week turnaround on most rugs.
  • Pet odor on rugsSame enzyme treatment as carpet. Pet stains in heirloom rugs come out properly.
  • Climate-controlled dryingRugs dry flat in a controlled environment. No sun bleaching, no shrinkage.

Free test spot first. Always. We never expect you to pay before you see what we can do. The Dirt Free Promise
