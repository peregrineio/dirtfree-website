The Tile & Grout process.

High-pressure cleaning combined with professional-grade solutions takes tile floors and grout lines back to like-new condition. We handle ceramic, porcelain, slate, and natural stone. Color restoration on dingy grout. Sealing on request.

  • High-pressure deep cleanTruck-powered pressure reaches into grout lines that mop water cannot touch.
  • Grout sealing availableOptional sealant locks color and resists future spills. Recommended every 2 to 3 years.
  • All tile types welcomeCeramic, porcelain, slate, travertine. We adjust pressure and chemistry to the surface.
  • Color restorationDiscolored grout often comes back to original color in a single visit. We test before we commit.
  • Steam rinsingRemoves the residue left by household tile cleaners that locks in dirt over time.
  • Free test areaSame policy as carpet. We clean a small area before we run the full floor.
