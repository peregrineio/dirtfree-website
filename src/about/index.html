Houston, since 1989

One truck. One patent.
One promise.

Aubrey Thoede started Dirt Free Carpet in 1989 with a single truck-mounted unit and a patent on an organic cleaner safe enough to drink. Thirty-five years later we are still that same company.

The founder

Aubrey Thoede.

Aubrey Thoede · Founder, 1989

Aubrey was a Houston carpet cleaning technician in the 1980s and he kept watching the same problem: a fresh-cleaned carpet would look great for two weeks and then look worse than before. The reason was the soap. Every cleaning company on the market used harsh detergents that left a sticky residue, and that residue was a magnet for dirt.

He spent two years developing a chemical-free alternative. The result was a patented organic cleaner that cleans without leaving anything in the fiber. Safe enough to drink. He proved that on live TV. Multiple times. Still would.

In 1989 he bought a single truck-mounted unit, mounted his patented cleaner system on it, and started Dirt Free Carpet. Houston was the test market. Houston is still the home market.

What that means today
  • Same familyAubrey still owns the company. Family-run, not corporate-owned.
  • Same patented cleanerThe organic compound he developed is still our exclusive cleaning solution. Not available on the open market.
  • Same truck-mounted equipmentDiesel-engine truck-mount unit, three hundred and fifty gallons of filtered water on board, custom-built machines.
  • Same free test spotEvery job since 1989 has started with a free test in your worst area before you owe anything.
  • Same HoustonWe are not a national franchise serving Houston from afar. We are Houston.
  • Same callback windowIf a spot returns within seven days of an approved test spot, we come back at no charge.
