The Dirt Free Process

Six steps. One free test spot before you pay.

Every job starts with a free test in your worst area. If we can't impress you there, we pack up and leave.

01

Free test spot

We start in your worst area at no charge. You see the result before we run a single room.

02

Prep & pre-treat

Certified techs assess carpet type, identify problem zones, pre-treat with non-toxic solutions.

03

Truck-powered steam

Diesel-engine truck-mount unit applies our patented organic cleaner and pulls dirt to the padding.

04

Steam rinse

This is the step competitors skip. We rinse all soap and detergent residue, even from previous cleaners.

05

Speed dry system

Free Speed Dry deployment cuts dry time to 1 to 6 hours instead of the typical 24 to 48.

06

Final walkthrough

We walk every room with you. Seven-day callback window covers any spot that returns.

Houston Heritage
Our story

Three and a half decades, one stretch of Houston, one promise.

Dirt Free Carpet started in 1989 with a single truck-mounted unit, a patent on an organic cleaning solution, and one rule: no soap, no detergent, nothing in the house we wouldn't drink ourselves. Aubrey demonstrated that on live TV. He still would.

Thirty-five years later we are the same company. Same family. Same chemical-free system. Same free test spot in your worst area before you pay a dime. The difference is fifteen thousand Houston homes have walked us through their door, and our truck-mounted equipment now reaches the entire greater Houston area in under an hour.
